About the Creator

:My name is Arpan Adhikari. I came to know about stoicism while wandering in search of mental clarity.

• Facing Adversity: Life was tough. I felt overwhelmed by challenges and emotions, unsure of how to handle everything that was happening.

• The Encounter with Stoicism: During this difficult period, I stumbled upon Marcus Aurelius. His thoughts on accepting what’s beyond our control and focusing on how we respond to things felt like a lifeline.

• The Fundamental Shift: Stoicism taught me to stop stressing over things I couldn’t change. Instead, I began focusing on what I could control—my reactions, thoughts, and actions.

• A New Way of Thinking: Challenges stopped feeling like setbacks. I started seeing them as opportunities to learn, grow, and become stronger.

• Emotional Mastery: I realized I didn’t have to be driven by every emotion that came my way. Stoicism helped me take a step back, think before reacting, and approach situations more calmly.

• Cultivating Resilience: Life’s difficulties didn’t disappear, but I became more resilient. Stoicism gave me the clarity and strength to face them without feeling completely lost.

• Embracing Growth: Over time, stoicism shaped how I viewed personal growth. It helped me become more grounded, focused, and intentional with my decisions and actions.
